Introduction

This page introduces the Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) and its evolution over time. HTTP is the foundation of data communication on the World Wide Web, enabling the transfer of hypertext documents and other resources between clients and servers. This page will explore the history of HTTP, its key concepts, and how it has evolved to meet the demands of modern web applications.
